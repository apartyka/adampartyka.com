Affiliate Platform Coordinator

Responsibilities:

  • The Affiliate Platform Coordinator is responsible for servicing, optimizing and growing our internal/external affiliate network(s) which generate substantial traffic within our desired target audiences
  • Recognize new marketing opportunities & stay current with trends in Affiliate Marketing
  • Organize and implement new affiliates
  • Monitor affiliate leads, optimize campaigns as needed and provide ongoing quality assurance support
  • Manage the day-to-day support of designated Affiliate networks as well as in-house affiliate platform
  • Make numerous outgoing calls each day to various affiliates, respond to emails, and manage customer support issues for affiliate team
  • Coordinate and approve Affiliate billing to ensure compliance with contracts and accuracy
  • Create weekly visuals/breakdowns of affiliate growth, including ROI, along with providing reasons and projections
  • Monitor conversions/affiliate performance, along with due commissions
  • Supervising and examining TOS (“Terms of Service”) abuses among other affiliate conflicts

Affiliate Experience and Skills Required for this Position:

  • Held a position in affiliate marketing – as an merchant/affiliate or with a network or agency
  • Knowledge of CPA, Co-reg, Lead Generations, Search and Email Marketing
  • Breadth of knowledge of affiliate platforms/networks
  • Quickly recognize and focus on the most potentially profitable affiliates
  • Critique and Optimize affiliate offerings on an individual and general basis
  • Ability to understand and verbalize technical language involved in affiliate implementation

Additional Requirements:

  • 2 - 4 years experience in a media planning/buying position at affiliate network or an advertising/media agency
  • Excellent verbal/written communication, presentation, customer service, and analytical skills
  • Confidence on the phone a must
  • Problem solving skills and the ability to learn quickly are critica
  • Able to capitalize on opportunities whenever and wherever presented
  • Possess strong project management skills, including demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Exhibit strong analytical skills, including the ability to analyze data, draw conclusions, and develop recommendations
  • Self starter who can work independently as well as within a team environment
  • Maintain familiarity with affiliate marketing trends
  • Knowledge using Microsoft Office products

Nice to have – but not Required:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or related field required
  • Enjoys dynamic environment and new interactive trends
  • A creative person that can multi-task, and has a high degree of attention to detail
  • Knowledge of online marketing campaign management
