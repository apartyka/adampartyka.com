Kevin Chern, President

When he joined Ed to help lead Infra-Strategy, Kevin Chern was no newcomer to growing businesses. From 1997 to 2005, he worked as managing partner of the country’s largest consumer bankruptcy law firm. The firm, which began with only two attorneys, included more than 180 employees in 19 states serving approximately 450 new clients weekly by the end of Kevin’s tenure. As a lawyer, he has represented thousands of bankruptcy filers and made a name for himself as an expert in law practice development.

Now the President of Infra-Strategy, Kevin has again used his expertise to help the company expand from its starting core to more than 100 employees. By contributing his expertise in the areas of law practice management, marketing, business development and technology, Kevin has helped Infra-Strategy provide its clients with the support and innovation needed to stay competitive in an online market.

As the author of Life after Bankruptcy, contributing writer to several legal blogs and a member of the National Association of Consumer Bankruptcy Attorneys (NACBA) and the American Bankruptcy Institute (ABI), Kevin uses his cutting-edge legal knowledge to improve the service and products Infra-Strategy provides its clients.
