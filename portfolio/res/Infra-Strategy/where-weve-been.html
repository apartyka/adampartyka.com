where we've been

2002

  • Ed Scanlan founds Infra-Strategy at 1846 N. Damen Ave., in his Chicago apartment above a Bucktown Italian ice stand.
  • Infra-Strategy moves its headquarters to downtown Chicago at 12 S. Michigan Ave., the then Chicago Athletic Association. The "office" is composed of two former hotel rooms with the interior wall knocked down (by Ed) and is accessible only by crossing a boiler room, the hotel’s fire exit or a men’s locker room. Five people work for the company.

2003

  • Infra-Strategy doubles its revenue and employee count.

2004

  • Because of continuing growth, Infra-Strategy moves again. The office is relocated down the street to the 29th floor in Willoughby Tower, at 8 S. Michigan Ave.

2005

  • Kevin Chern , previously a managing partner with the nation’s largest consumer bankruptcy firm, joins Infra-Strategy as president and Ed’s partner. At this time, the company has a dozen employees and launches TotalAttorneys.com.
  • Infra-IT is developed, becomes a section of the company with an IT business focus and transitions from charging hourly rates to becoming a monthly service provider.
  • For the third year in a row, both Infra-Strategy’s head count and its revenue double.

2006

  • As the team expands, Infra-Strategy spreads through Willoughby Tower, now occupying the 13th, 27th, 29th and 30th floors.
  • Once again, revenue and the employee head count more than double.

2007

  • Infra-Strategy moves to the 5th floor of 25 E. Washington St., and—for the fifth year in a row—the company expands its revenue and its number of employees by more than double from the year before.

2008

  • Infra-Strategy is named the 169th fastest growing private company in the United States by Inc. magazine’s 2008 Inc. 500 list.
  • Infra is ranked No. 8 in the Top 100 Businesses in the Chicago and Tri-State Area and No. 23 in the Top 100 Business Services Companies in the country.
  • Infra-IT is sold so the company can focus on the wildly successful TotalAttorneys.com business.
  • Infra-Strategy’s TotalAttorneys.com launches a new service offering pilot, legal process outsourcing with 15 consumer law firms.
